About Tinnos Farm Trip Company

Tinnos Farm Trip Company is dedicated to promoting sustainable agriculture and empowering local farmers through educational tours and training programs.

Our mission is to foster climate-smart farming practices that enhance productivity while preserving the environment. We offer a range of services including:

  • Guided farm tours showcasing organic Potato, spinach, and sukuma wiki cultivation
  • Hands-on training sessions with local farmers on best agricultural practices
  • Sales of organic produce directly from our farms to consumers

At Tinnos Farm Trip Company, we believe in the power of knowledge-sharing and community engagement to drive positive change in agriculture. Whether you're a visitor interested in sustainable farming or a farmer seeking to improve your techniques, we welcome you to join us on our journey towards a healthier planet.
